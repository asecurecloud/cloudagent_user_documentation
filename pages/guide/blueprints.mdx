## Objective
Design, visualize, and deploy standardized infrastructure templates across your environments.

The **Blueprint Builder** allows you to design and deploy infrastructure templates.

To access it:
1.  Click on **My Blueprints** in the left sidebar.
2.  You will see a list of your existing blueprints (or a message if you haven't created any yet).
3.  Click **Create Blueprint** to start a new design.
